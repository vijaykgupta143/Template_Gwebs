HOW IT WORK

Connect

Signup, buy tokens, and you are all set! Tokens allow you to run our cryptocurrency arbitrage service for x amount of trades. Connect your exchanges to our platform with your secret trade key.

Select the exchanges, coins and tickers you want to trade on and wait for our algorithm to find a trade for you!

The algorithm will open a trade when two different tickers of the same coin have a significant price spread. It will close them once the profit threshold is reached.
